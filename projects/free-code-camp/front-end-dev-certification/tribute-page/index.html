Elvin Jones: Jazz Giant

Here's a time line of Elvin's life:

  • 1927 - Born in Pontiac, Michigan
  • 1940 - Joined his high school's black marching band, where he developed his foundation in rudiments
  • 1946 - Began service in the United States Army
  • 1949 - Discharged from the military and borrowed $35 from his sister to purchase a drumset
  • 1955 - Joined Charles Mingus and his big band in New York after being rejected by Benny Goodman's band
  • 1960 - Joined the John Coltrane Quartet with Jimmy Garrison and McCoy Tyner
  • 1966 - Left the quartet
  • 1968 - Formed a trio with Joe Farrell and Jimmy Garrison and continued to lead many other groups from then on
  • 2004 - dies at the age of 76.
